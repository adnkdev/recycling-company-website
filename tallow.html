Tallow Supply & Collection

AARecycler provides consistent-quality tallow streams suitable for oleochemical, industrial, and renewable fuel applications across Victoria.

What is Tallow?

Tallow is a rendered animal fat widely used in industrial, manufacturing, and renewable fuel production. AARecycler aggregates, grades, and supplies bulk tallow volumes for commercial buyers across Victoria.

Applications
  • Renewable diesel & biodiesel feedstock
  • Oleochemicals & soap manufacturing
  • Industrial lubricants
  • Animal nutrition & feed blending
Tallow Grades Available
  • Edible grade
  • Technical grade
  • Industrial grade
  • Custom blends on request
Supply Volumes
  • Bulk container loads
  • IBC / drum supply
  • Regular contracted volumes
  • Spot purchasing available
