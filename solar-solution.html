Solar & Battery Energy Solutions

AARecycler designs integrated solar panel and battery systems that help businesses cut daytime electricity bills, reduce peak demand charges, and improve resilience to grid interruptions.

From simple rooftop solar installs to hybrid solar-plus-storage setups, we focus on practical systems that are matched to your load profile and site.

One Integrated Solar Solution

Rather than treating solar panels and batteries as separate products, AARecycler designs systems where each component works together. Panels generate during the day, batteries store excess energy, and your site draws from the system when it makes the most financial and operational sense.

Solar Panels

High-efficiency solar modules suitable for commercial roofs, warehouses, and industrial sheds, sized to match your daytime usage and available roof space.

  • Tier-1 panel brands
  • String and hybrid inverter options
  • Roof layouts tailored to your site
  • Performance and product warranties
Battery Storage

Lithium and hybrid battery systems that capture excess solar and provide power during evening peaks, tariff changes, or selected grid outages.

  • Scalable storage capacity
  • Hybrid & off-grid ready configurations
  • Backup supply for critical loads (where suitable)
  • Monitoring & control via smart platforms

3. Proposal & Options

4. Install & Support

Accredited installers deliver the project, with ongoing support for monitoring, warranty, and optimisation.
