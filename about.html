About AARecycler

A Victorian-owned partner helping businesses turn waste into value through circular economy and renewable energy solutions.

Our Mission

AARecycler was founded with a simple objective: create a direct link between waste recovery and clean energy outcomes. We provide reliable tallow and used cooking oil recycling, while delivering accessible solar and battery systems for businesses across Victoria.

  • • Reduce industrial waste across food and rendering sectors
  • • Support the production of renewable fuels and clean energy
  • • Promote sustainable, cost-effective solutions for businesses
  • • Build partnerships that prioritise traceability and compliance

What We Do

Tallow & Oil Recycling

We collect, aggregate and supply tallow and used cooking oil for renewable fuel, oleochemical, and industrial applications.

  • Traceable collection
  • Consistent quality control
  • Bulk volumes available
Solar Panels & Batteries

Helping businesses lower energy costs and become more self-reliant through modern solar PV and battery storage systems.

  • Tier-1 solar modules
  • Hybrid & off-grid systems
  • Professional installation
Victorian-Wide Service

Based in Victoria, we support clients across metro and regional areas with fast collection, delivery, and installation.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Reliable turnaround times
  • Commercial-grade support
