Recycling & Renewable Energy Products

We support both circular economy and clean energy outcomes with reliable offtake for tallow and used cooking oil, as well as supply of modern solar panels and battery storage systems.

Tallow

Collected tallow is aggregated and prepared for refining, oleochemical, and renewable fuel applications.

  • Consistent quality streams
  • Suitable for biodiesel & industrial use
  • Bulk volumes available
Used Cooking Oil (UCO)

UCO is collected, stored, and supplied as a feedstock for renewable fuels and other industrial processes.

  • Food-grade collection equipment
  • Traceable supply chain
  • Biofuel-ready material
Solar Panels

High-efficiency solar modules suitable for residential roofs, warehouses, factories, and small commercial sites.

  • Tier-1 panel brands
  • System design options
  • Warranty and performance support
Solar Batteries

Lithium and hybrid battery systems that store excess solar and provide backup during peak pricing or outages.

  • Scalable storage sizes
  • Hybrid & off-grid ready
  • Compatible with major inverters
