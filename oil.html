Used Cooking Oil Collection & Supply

AARecycler provides clean, traceable used cooking oil (UCO) collection and bulk supply for renewable fuel and industrial applications across Victoria.

What is Used Cooking Oil (UCO)?

Used cooking oil is a valuable feedstock for renewable fuels and a range of industrial processes. AARecycler works with food producers, restaurants, and processors to safely collect, store, and supply UCO as a reliable raw material.

Typical Sources
  • Restaurants and commercial kitchens
  • Food manufacturers and processors
  • QSR and franchise groups
  • Industrial fryers and renderers
Key Applications
  • Renewable diesel & biodiesel production
  • Oleochemical manufacturing
  • Industrial lubricants and additives
  • Other chemical processing
Collection Options
  • Scheduled collections
  • On-call pickups
  • Drum, IBC or bulk storage
  • Multi-site collection programs

Handling & Quality Standards

Food-Grade Collection Equipment

We provide sealed collection vessels, drums, or IBCs designed to minimise spills, contamination, and odour, ensuring safe on-site handling.

Filtered & Settled Supply

Collected UCO is filtered and settled to reduce solids and water content, supporting efficient downstream processing and fuel conversion.

Traceable Supply Chain

Each batch is tracked from collection through to delivery, providing documentation that supports compliance and certification requirements.

Flexible Volumes

From smaller regular collections to bulk export-ready loads, we tailor our service to the scale of your operations.

For Sites Generating Used Cooking Oil

Simple Onboarding

We review your current oil handling, propose collection points, and set a schedule that minimises disruption to your kitchen or production line.

Clean & Safe Work Areas

Proper UCO management reduces spill risk, odours, and blocked drains, helping maintain a safer, cleaner work environment.

Sustainability Outcomes

Partnering with AARecycler helps divert waste from landfill and supports the production of lower-carbon fuels and products.
